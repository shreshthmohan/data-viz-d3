Tax variation across S&P 500 companies (2007-2012)

  • Recreated in d3.js v7 (original was in v3) from a 2013 NYT article: Across U.S. Companies, Tax Rates Vary Greatly
  • This chart has the force simulation result from the other variant.
  • A Voronoi layer is also present for a better interaction experience. Use the checkbox in the top-right of the chart to see the Voronoi. This makes it easier to hover over small circles. Even moving the mouse pointer close to a circle whose info you wish to see activates the tooltip. The pointer doesn't have to be exactly over the circle.
